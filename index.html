What is Garuda.ai ?

The Garuda AI Bot is a virtual assistant designed to guide and inform users, making various tasks more manageable. Named after Garuda, a bird often referred to as the "King of Birds," it symbolizes loyalty, security, and friendliness, reflecting the bot's core values and purpose.

What is the use of Garuda.ai ?

Garuda.ai is a sophisticated AI-integrated chatbot designed to assist citizens in resolving their queries efficiently without the need for direct interaction with law enforcement personnel. It aims to provide a seamless and professional user experience, ensuring clarity and convenience while addressing concerns.

What are the features included in Garuda.ai ?

Online FIR filing for seamless reporting of incidents, Real-time FIR process status updates to keep citizens informed, Appointment scheduling for streamlined interactions, News and alerts to ensure timely dissemination of important information, An AI-integrated chatbot for addressing queries with precision and convenience.

Is my data safe with Garuda.ai

Garuda.ai ensures that your data is handled with the highest standards of safety and security. With robust measures and advanced technology in place, the platform guarantees the protection of your information, offering you peace of mind while using its services.

Does Garuda.ai include mobile responsiveness ?

Yes! Garuda.ai is fully responsive and optimized for all devices, ensuring a seamless user experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
